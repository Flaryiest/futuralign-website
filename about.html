Our Aim
Futurlign aims to promote discussion and education surrounding AI, emphasizing the importance of alignment in its development and integration. We believe in equipping individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the opportunities and challenges presented by AI.
What We Do
Through a variety of initiatives, including workshops, seminars, and community events, we engage with students, professionals, and the public to explore the multifaceted aspects of AI.
Our Vision
We envision a future where AI is developed and deployed in a manner that aligns with human values and serves the collective good.
