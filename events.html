Artificial Intelligence DECODED

Artificial Intelligence DECODED is a 3-hour workshop hosted in libaries around Calgary for teens interested in AI. We will delve into how AI works, applications of AI, and potential challenges with fun activities and discussions. Workshops will run through July and August, both on weekdays and on weekends.
Schedule and Information
Note there may be changes in time and dates based on room availibility. Workshops taking place on weekdays will run from 5:00-8:00 P.M. and workshops on weekends will run from 1:00-4:00 P.M.

AI Course
Futurlign hosted a Python and ML course at the University of Calgary, targeted towards youth between grades 6-9. Within this course, individuals were able to gain insight into the inner workings of AI, learn how to program in Python, and create a miniature AI themselves.
